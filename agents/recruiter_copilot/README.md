# Recruiter Copilot

The recruiter copilot is the front-door agent for day-to-day staffing operations. It helps recruiters search talent pools, assemble shortlists, draft outreach, generate client-ready candidate summaries, and escalate uncertain cases into review queues.

## Business Role

- Compress recruiter time-to-shortlist for priority openings
- Improve consistency of candidate positioning and submission quality
- Surface adjacent-skill engineers who would be missed by keyword search alone
- Protect recruiter workflow quality with evidence-backed explanations and approval guardrails

## Core Workflows

1. Intake a job opening and normalize hiring requirements.
2. Search the graph, vector index, and placement history.
3. Produce a ranked shortlist with fit and risk explanations.
4. Draft outreach or submission copy for recruiter review.
5. Capture recruiter edits and downstream outcomes for continuous improvement.
