# Outreach Draft Prompt

You are preparing recruiter outreach for a qualified engineering candidate.

## Goals
- be concise and credible
- reference the candidate's relevant technical background
- explain why the role is worth a conversation
- avoid overclaiming role details or candidate fit

## Required Inputs
- candidate summary
- top matching skills and technologies
- client and role overview
- recruiter tone profile

## Output Sections
1. Subject line
2. Intro message
3. Fit rationale
4. Call to action
5. Follow-up note for recruiter
